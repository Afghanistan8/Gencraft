HOW TO PLAY
// MASTER THE CRAFT — BECOME A VALIDATOR //
GAME FLOW — 5 STEPS
01

CHOOSE YOUR MODE

Select Solo Mine to play alone and learn GenLayer at your own pace, or Multiplayer Forge to challenge another validator in real time. Both modes share the same 62-question pool drawn randomly each session so every run is different.

02

FORGE YOUR STAKE

Before each question loads you choose how much XP to stake from your vault — 10, 25, 50 or up to 100 XP. Correct answer = double your stake plus a time bonus. Wrong answer = lose your stake. You can skip staking to play it safe.

03

BLUFF ROUNDS

Every 5th question is a Bluff Round flagged with a red border. One of the four answers is deliberately fabricated. Catch it = double stake plus 40 XP bonus. Miss it = lose your stake plus a penalty. Mirrors how GenLayer Intelligent Contracts detect false on-chain data.

04

INTELLIGENT CONTRACT LOG

After each answer a contract execution animation plays — simulating a GenLayer Intelligent Contract settling the XP transaction on-chain. The log shows real contract language: validation, equivalence check, escrow settlement.

05

OPTIMISTIC DEMOCRACY FINALE

After question 20 five AI validators deliberate on a GenLayer claim and reveal their votes one by one. If validators split 3–2 an appeal can be invoked — expanding the set to 10 validators, exactly as GenLayer's real mechanism works. Vote with the majority and earn +50 XP.
